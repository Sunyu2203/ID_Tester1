    Brief summary of THE BAND

    First rising to stardom in 2015 with their song "Rushing till midnight", this Singaporean band has gotten much praise locally and internationally. THE BAND, which consists of 3 members, Theo 'T' Lim, Hilde 'H' Tan and Evan 'E' Cho have toured regionally and hopes to tour the globe soon.
